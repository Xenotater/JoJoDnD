Patch Notes

Below is a list of patch notes for changes made to the website and module. It should be noted some smaller and less relevant changes are not listed. For those curious about every detailed change to the site, the source code and change history is available on GitHub.

1/27/2024

Update 1.12.3

  • A few important balance changes!
    • Stands can use their Damage Reduction on damage from some physical Saving Throws now
    • Charmed creatures can have a free Charisma Check made against them each Turn
    • Familiar can't be killed unless their owner dies
    • Melee Barrage was nerfed slightly, no longer able to use Stand stats for the DC
    • Ranged Attack was rebalanced, also unable to use Stand stats for its burst mode's DC and dealing more damage with its focused mode
  • A number of small but important clarifications
    • The 'guide' that we recommend Custom Abilities follow (on the main Stands page) has been reworked quite a bit
    • There are defined rules for protecting someone else with your Stand on the main Stands page now
    • Energy Drain from certain Undead now has defined rules about a person who wasn't fully drained recovering from being drained.
    • Passive abilities are often indiscriminate AOEs
    • Hunker (the weapon attribute) only grants cover in one direction
    • Vampires ascending via the Completed Mask is now a Variant Rule, since there's no canon example of this
  • A new Ability was added for Non-Supernatural Classes: Signature Move
    • This should give them a personally customized move to keep up with the supernatural classes in terms of their 'cool factor'
  • Several new Feats: Combination Ability, Equalizer, and Substand Expert
    • Combination Ability is for Multiclassers and Undead, allowing you to combine your classes' features in a unique skill
    • Equalizer is a way to allow those without a Stand to have a better chance of fighting a Stand User, letting them apply pressure even with mundane weapons
      • Anyone with a Supernatural Item (Non-Super Variant Rule) gains this, as does Remote and Revenge Type
    • Substand expert lets you briefly use a Stand even while the other is summoned/active, similar to the use of Epitaph and King Crimson in canon
  • Suit Stand was completely reworked
    • It now functions differently depending on your Stand Type, and Ranged Types ARE allowed to take it once again
  • A few small Class changes:
    • Non-Supernatural Classes get the new Signature Move Ability at Level 3
    • Hive had Swarm Attacks were further clarified, its DC changed and it can't be resisted with Stand stats, Attacking and Defending with half or less of your Instances was clarified
    • Remote and Revenge Types gain the new Equalizer Ability at Level 1
    • Ripple and Spin get their Custom Techniques at Level 3 instead of Level 5
    • Modular Spin gets two features at Levels 5 and 6, to keep up with the non-modular variant
  • A number of Abilities were tweaked, changed, or clarified in smaller ways, be sure to look over the following if your character uses them:
    • Act 0, Artisan Fighting Style, Artisan's Resurgence, Confident Provocation, Heavyweight Fighting Style, Immense Power, Improved Immense Range, Melee Barrage, Natural Armor, Perfected Survivalist, Ranged Attack, Ranger Fighting Style, Ranger's Recovery, Ripple Feature, Special Ability, Stand Energy Recharge, Supernatural Item, Survivalist, Warrior Fighting Style, Zombification
  • A number of Feats were tweaked, changed, or clarified in smaller ways, be sure to look over the following if your character uses them:
    • Additional Sense, Adrenaline Rush, Aura of Awareness, Brutality, Combat Medic, Commentator, Crusher, Elementalist, Extra Experience, Heckler, Feel No Pain, Heavy Sleeper, Object Stand, Perfected Craft, Piercer, Powerful Presence, Practical Experience, Sentient Stand, Slasher, Specialized Save, Suit Stand
  • Familiars now have an Extra Sense Feat
    • This works similarly to Additional Sense, though slightly more limited
  • A number of other small fixes and clarifications
    • Many of these were from the community's issue report channel in our discord, so thank you all and be sure to let us know if we missed anything!
10/19/2024
Website Update v1.12.2.4
  • New Act-Type page in the Editor
    • Act-Type Stands function very differently from others, having essentially 3-4 separate Stands with unique stats and Abilities. Because of this, they never worked very well within the Editor. To correct this, we've now added another page just for them.
    • This new page contains space for all of your Act's stats and info in one place, and only shows up when "Act" is selected as your class.
      • This does mean that it won't work for multiclassing. I may consider having the page show up when the "Other/Multiclass" option is selected, but for the time being I don't think that's necessary. We really don't recommend trying to multiclass or multitype with Act-Type anyway.
    • It should be noted that we removed support for Act 0 in the sheet, as they don't really have much stat-wise anyway and cease to exist when you get Act 1. We've also added support for Act 4. Because of this, your existing Act data may be shifted over by 1 after this update, but it will all still be there even if it's in the wrong place on the sheet.
      • This page will be added to the PDF version in its next update, and should be added within roll20 very soon and TTS at some point.
  • A few more Editor tweaks
    • The autofill toggle has been moved away from the page toggles to be more prominent, now being alongside the stat-flip toggle.
    • Fixed a bug relating to searching within the load character menu.
  • Fancied up the social buttons on the homepage
    • They now look nicer and display our total and active members

Website Update v1.12.1.7
  • A few optimizations and tweaks to the editor
