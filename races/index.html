Races

Keep in Mind:

Most characters in the world of JoJo's are Human, but more exotic Races can occasionally be found.
Races under the "NPC Races" label aren't balanced for players, generally being much weaker or stronger than the Player Races, but can still technically be played by players at the DMs descretion.
