Classes

Multiclassing:

You may choose to gain levels in more than one of these classes, but can only level up in one at a time. For example, you may become a Ripple User that also has a Stand, but you'll need to choose which class to gain in for each level-up. Races with levels do not have this restriction, and their levels work differently. Check the respective Race page for details.
