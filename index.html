A Module for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition based upon JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

Including Stand Users, Vampires, Pillar Men, and more, this extensive module for D&D 5th Edition allows the creation of your very own Bizarre Adventure!

Use the tabs at the top of the page to navigate the module's resources and create a world filled with characters with any of the races and abilities from JoJo's.

New to the module? Start with the Rules page!

Written by Maggy Bledsoe, playtested by Hayden C., Kyler Froman, Donovan Hord, Thalia Huebner, and Ty Ostrander, edited by Kyler Froman and Carol C.
