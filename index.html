Including Stand Users, Vampires, Pillar Men, and more, this extensive module for D&D 5th Edition allows the creation of your very own Bizarre Adventure!

Use the tabs at the top of the page to navigate the module's resources and create a world filled with characters of any of the races from JoJo's and give them special abilities like Hamon, a Stand with custom abilities, or knowledge of the Spin.

New to the module? Start with the Rules page!



Written by MD Bledsoe and playtested by Hayden Camp, Kyler Froman, Donovan Hord, Tyson Huebner, Asra Kennon, and Ty Ostrander.
